Field Trips   

Free field trip program for students!

Free field trips for groups of 10 students or more

Exceptions on Saturdays and Sundays after 12:00 PM

Program includes:

  • teacher's lesson packet specific to each grade level
  • round trip ride on the Duquesne Incline
  • history of inclines in Pittsburgh
  • discussion about Pittsburgh's industrial past
  • visit to the machine room to observe the original hoisting equipment in operation and learn about the engineers who built it
  • panoramic view of Downtown Pittsburgh and The North Shore from our observation deck 400 feet above the three rivers
  • brief history of the French and Indian War from which the city arose
